Jennifer Aniston

  • 1969
  • Jennifer Joanna Aniston was born in Sherman Oaks, Los Angeles
  • 1975
  • She was admitted to Waldorf School and then started acting at the age of eleven. Later, she enrolled at Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts, where she graduated. In her twenties Jennifer was diagnosed with dyslexia. Until then, she was suffering from low self-esteem. The diagnosis helped her to understand her shortcoming. She soon overcame all her troubles.
  • 1988
  • She started her acting career by working in Off Broadway shows. In the same year she moved to Los Angeles
  • 1990
  • She was cast in her first television serial, ‘Molloy’
  • 1993
  • Jennifer Aniston made her film debut in ‘Leprechaun’, a horror film that received mostly negative reviews
  • 1994
  • Her breakthrough! ‘Friends’ was aired on NBC. Jennifer played the part of Rachel Green. The show was a huge success. After that, she also acted in a number of successful films
  • 2000
  • Jennifer Aniston married actor Brad Pitt after two years of courtship. However, the marriage broke down within five years. The couple finalized their divorce in 2005
  • 2002
  • She founded a film production company named Plan B Entertainment. It was joint venture with Brad Pitt and Brad Grey. Later she withdrew from it to form Echo Films, another production company, with Kristin Hahn. Besides, she is the co-owner of a hair care company called ‘Living Proof’
  • 2003
  • Jennifer Aniston took home her first Golden Globe for her work in Friends
  • 2007
  • Jennifer Aniston won Vanguard Award for her role in promoting equal rights for LGBT people. She has also contributed appreciably for the cause of lesbians, gays, bisexuals and transgender (LGBT) and has hosted shows like Stand Up For Cancer and It Can’t Wait.
  • 2011
  • Jennifer began seeing Justin Theroux. The couple became engaged on August 10, 2012 and married after three years on August 5, 2015. They separated at the end of 2017
